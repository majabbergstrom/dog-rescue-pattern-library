Who Are We?

LOYAL Rescue is a foster based rescue. Every dog under our wing lives in a foster home where they gain the necessary knowledge, skills, and love to succeed with a forever family. Successful applicants are welcome to visit their potential new best friend, but viewing hours are not open to public. Fostering or adopting is not an obligation, be sure that you are capable of caring for the pet. Look at all of the wonderful dogs available for adoption, or view dogs who have previously been in our care.
